USER MANUAL

IdentaMaster files and folders cryptography allows you biometrically control access to sensitive information on your Windows® account.

To start the encryption procedure select the file, group of files and/or folders and right click. Then select'BioSecure' from the drop down menu.


A dialogue box pops up and prompts you to identify yourself by selecting one of the available biometric scanners. Place your finger on the scanner until your fingerprint image is accepted; this may take more tham one try. Also, if you don't want to keep the original files or folders without biometric protection, check 'Delete original file/folder after encryption'.




Once the user successfully identifies, the encryption procedure starts. A window will display the encryption progress on each item.




After the encryption procedure is completed, the protected package is saved with the extension .izbiosecure




To start decryption, right click on your biometrically secured file/foler (file .izbiosecure) and select 'BioSecure'




You will be prompted to begin the identification procedure; select your biometric device and scan your finger. Also, you may select option to overwrite original data: if current folder already contains files or folders, they will be restored from your archive after decryption finished and it replaces existing old data. Otherwise, system unpacks the data and create its copy.




After successful identification, the user will see a window showing the contents of the encrypted archive and prompted to either decrypt and extract all of its content or just particular item or items:




You may monitor decryption progress. The time of decryption depends on the amount of data in your saved archive.




The decrypted documents will be restored in the same folder where the encrypted data have been placed.
